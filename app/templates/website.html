Everything you need to know before you start.

TradeHub is a strategy builder and marketplace for crypto traders. Whether you're a complete beginner or experienced quant, you can describe a trading idea in plain English, let the AI compile it into a live strategy, paper-test it on real market data, and then automate it or sell it in the marketplace.
Not at all. You describe your strategy in plain English — entry signals, exit targets, leverage, risk rules — and the AI handles everything else. No coding, no spreadsheets, no technical setup required.
Yes, completely free with no time limit. Build as many strategies as you want, run them on live market data in paper mode, and only upgrade to live automation when you're confident in your results. No credit card required to start.
Once you're happy with a strategy's paper results, you can switch it to live mode on your Pro plan. The platform connects to your exchange account and executes trades automatically 24/7 — entries, exits, take profits, and stop losses — all handled for you.
The built-in backtester replays your strategy against real 1-hour OHLCV candle data. It covers all USDT perpetual pairs including low-cap and trending coins. Results include win rate, total P&L, profit factor, max drawdown, average hold time, and a full trade log.
Any user can publish a strategy to the marketplace and set their own price. When another trader buys or subscribes, you receive 80% of the revenue automatically. Performance stats shown on your listing are verified directly from live and paper trading data — not self-reported.
Pro unlocks live trade automation, unlimited AI chat with the strategy advisor, priority signal routing, and full access to all marketplace strategies. Free accounts can still build unlimited strategies, paper test indefinitely, publish to the marketplace, and use the AI builder.
TradeHub never holds your funds. Live automation connects to your own exchange account via API keys with trade-only permissions — we cannot withdraw or transfer your assets. Your funds stay on your exchange at all times.
Live automation currently supports Bitunix Futures (USDT perpetuals). Paper testing and backtesting cover all major USDT perp pairs sourced from multiple data providers. Additional exchange integrations are on the roadmap.
Create a free account in seconds — no credit card needed. Then use the 7-step strategy wizard or just describe your idea in the AI chat. Your strategy is live in paper mode within minutes. When you're ready to go live, upgrade to Pro.
Once you're on a Pro plan, go to your account settings inside the portal and click Connect Exchange. You'll be prompted to enter your Bitunix API Key and Secret. These are created inside your Bitunix account under API Management — generate a new key, copy both values, and paste them in. The connection is verified instantly.
You only need to enable Futures Trading (read + trade). Never enable withdrawals. TradeHub only needs permission to open and close positions — it cannot move funds off your exchange. We also strongly recommend restricting the API key to a specific IP address for extra security.
Your API keys are encrypted with AES-256 before being stored and are never exposed in plain text. We use Fernet encryption and never log key values. As an extra precaution, always create a dedicated API key just for TradeHub — never share a key that also has withdrawal access.
You can disconnect your exchange at any time from your account settings — this immediately stops all live automation. To rotate keys, delete the existing key on Bitunix, generate a new one, and update it in your TradeHub settings. All running strategies will pause until the new key is saved.
TradeHub only manages positions it opens itself. Manual trades you place directly on the exchange are untouched. However, we recommend keeping TradeHub strategies on a sub-account or with a separate allocation so your manual trading and automated strategies don't conflict.
