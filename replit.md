# TradeHub Markets — Strategy Builder Platform

## Overview
TradeHub Markets is a web platform designed for users to build, test, and automate crypto trading strategies without requiring any coding knowledge. Users can create strategies using a 7-step wizard or an AI chat builder, paper test them for free, and then deploy them live or sell them in a marketplace. The platform operates on a Free vs. Pro ($50/month) subscription model. A companion Telegram bot provides strategy trade alerts and user login UIDs. The business vision is to empower traders with accessible, sophisticated tools, expanding market potential by democratizing strategy creation and automation in the crypto space.

The engine in `app/services/backtest_engine.py` models real-trading frictions explicitly: (1) **fees** 0.10% round-trip taker × leverage; (2) **slippage** 0.04% round-trip × leverage on every market fill, plus an extra 0.05% gap penalty when a bar opens past the SL/TP trigger; (3) **funding** continuous accrual at 0.01% per 8h × leverage based on actual hold time in minutes; (4) **liquidation** isolated-margin liq price computed at entry as `entry × (1 − (1 − maint/100) / leverage)` with maint=0.5%, checked on every bar's wick BEFORE TP/SL — fires only when liq sits inside the SL distance (catches absurd configs like SL 30% at 10× leverage), PnL clamped at −100%; (5) **gap-on-open** if the next bar opens past TP/SL, the trade exits at the gapped open price (worse for SL, better for TP) with the 0.05% gap-slippage penalty added; (6) **same-bar TP+SL resolution** uses bar-color heuristic (green bar → wick went down-then-up so SL hit first for LONG; red bar → up-then-down so TP hit first for LONG; doji → conservative SL); (7) **multiplicative compounding** — equity is treated like a real account where each trade's % return is applied to the previous balance, so `total_pnl` is true compounded return and `max_drawdown` reflects asymmetric loss math (50% loss needs 100% gain to recover).
